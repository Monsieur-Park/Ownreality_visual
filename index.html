OwnReality. To Each His Own Reality

 From 2010 to 2016, the project treats the notions of the real and reality in the discourse on art in France, the FRG, the GDR and Poland between 1960 and 1989. The visualizations in this page were realized in the context of reusability and information retrieval of the research data, while it was focused on a dataset which
displays the chronology of exhibitions that were recorded during the period. The front image represents a visualized database schema from the graph database Neo4j. The colors of each nodes and edges identify the classification of the element and his relationship.
